About Me

I like nature, plants, and phytochemistry. Currently, I am working at Charles Universtity, where I focus on isolation of secondary metabolites of plants which could be used in the therapy of major civilization diseases like Alzheimer's disease or cancer. In my free time, I love learning to code in python or JavaScript for web development. I also play music on several instruments. When I am resting, I like to hangout with my friedns, watch movies, play videogames, or wander in nature.
